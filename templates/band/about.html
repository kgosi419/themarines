About themarines

We are themarines, a POP band formed in 2023. Our music blends various genres, including rock, pop, and blues.

Our journey began in a small garage. Made up of four members, led by our frontman Loyiso, we have become the face of blue-eyed soul in the 21st century. With hard work and passion, we have been bringing our sound to audiences around the world.

Join us as we continue to create, perform, and inspire through music!
